Data Protection & Privacy

Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Kevin Lin ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you use my Chrome extensions, web applications, and related services (collectively, "Services"). Your privacy is important to me, and I am committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Collection and Use

Types of Information Collected

Depending on the specific extension or service, I may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the extension, including features used and frequency of use
  • Technical Data: Browser version, extension version, operating system information for compatibility and debugging purposes
  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure within the extension
  • Error Reports: Crash reports and error logs to improve service stability (anonymized when possible)

How Information is Collected

  • Directly from You: When you configure settings, provide feedback, or contact support
  • Automatically: Through normal extension operation, analytics, and error reporting
  • Third-Party Services: When extensions integrate with external APIs or services

Use of Information

I use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the functionality of my extensions
  • Improving user experience and adding new features
  • Debugging and fixing technical issues
  • Analyzing usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Communicating with users about updates or support issues
  • Ensuring security and preventing misuse

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most user data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs. This includes:

  • Extension settings and preferences
  • User-generated content and configurations
  • Cached data for performance optimization

Remote Storage

Some extensions may store data remotely for synchronization or backup purposes. When this occurs:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Access is restricted and monitored
  • Data retention policies are clearly defined
  • Users are notified of remote storage usage

Data Sharing and Third Parties

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help operate my extensions (e.g., analytics, cloud storage)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with user notification)
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Third-Party Services

My extensions may integrate with third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google APIs and Services
  • GitHub API
  • Analytics services (when used)
  • Cloud storage providers

User Rights and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data is collected and how it's used
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and operational requirements)
  • Portability: Request export of your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable data collection features where technically feasible

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights, you can:

  • Use built-in privacy controls within the extension
  • Uninstall the extension to stop all data collection
  • Contact me directly using the information provided below
  • Clear extension data through Chrome's extension management page

Children's Privacy

My services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If I become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, I will take steps to remove such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. I ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers, including compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Data Retention

I retain information for the following periods:

  • User Settings: Until you uninstall the extension or clear the data
  • Usage Analytics: Up to 24 months for service improvement
  • Error Logs: Up to 12 months for debugging purposes
  • Support Communications: Up to 3 years for reference and follow-up

Updates to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When I do, I will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users of significant changes through the extension or via email
  • Provide a summary of changes when material updates are made
  • Maintain previous versions for reference when requested

Compliance and Certifications

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

  • Other applicable privacy and data protection laws
